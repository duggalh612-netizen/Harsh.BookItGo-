Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I find trains between two stations?
You can simply enter the departure and arrival stations in the search box. The app will show you all available trains connecting those stations along with their schedules.
2. How can I check the live running status of a train?
Enter the train number or name. The app will display the current location, expected arrival, and departure times at upcoming stations.
3. How long will the delay information be available if a train is late?
The app continuously updates the delay information as provided by official sources. You can track the delay status in real-time until the train reaches its destination.
4. How can I know the expected arrival and departure time at each station?
Once you select the train, you can view its route details. This includes scheduled arrival and departure times at each stop, as well as updated times in case of delays.
5. How do I check the route and stoppage details of a train?
After searching for the train, you can click on the route option. The app will show all the stations the train passes through, along with halt durations and other relevant information.
